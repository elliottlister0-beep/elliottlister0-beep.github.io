About Calico Aquatics

Calico Aquatics is a small coral and second-hand aquarium equipment shop based in Cumbria. We focus on healthy, well-kept coral and reliable pre-owned tanks and gear at fair prices. Everything we sell is checked, cleaned, and ready to use. Whether you're starting a reef tank or looking for extra equipment, we aim to make the marine hobby simpler and more affordable for everyone.

What We Sell

  • Professional aquarium installation (custom and standard sizes)
  • Quality pre‑owned tanks and stands
  • Live & decorative coral (subject to availability)
  • Filtration, lighting, and essential equipment
